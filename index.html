Hive61 is Poznan's Startup and Business Community.
It's a series of meetups for people with entrepreneurial drive in Poznań.

Hive is a dynamic entrepreneurial community. We invite everyone related with based in Poznan startups, businesses and any creative organizations.

From time to time we will be organizing meetings, preceded by valuable presentations given by known speakers. Each will take place in different location - because it's hive and it's not connected to one location.

You ask why "61"? Simple - it comes from Poznań post and area codes :)

Often presentations will be held in English - we find it important to have such an event in Poznań.
